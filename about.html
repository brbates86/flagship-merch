Our Story

Born on the Open Road

Flagship Merch isn't a merch agency — it's a crew. Rugged. Creative. Independent. WEAR THE FLAG.

Philosophy

More Than a Logo on a Shirt

We started where most good things do — in a garage, at a show, on a board, with a sketch and a screen. Flagship Merch draws from vintage nautical culture, tattoo flash, skate graphics, surf brands, hot rod culture, and the grit of real workwear.

Every drop is designed to feel like something you'd grab at a surf shop, skate shop, or creative market — not something that slid out of a corporate template. We print for bands, crews, local businesses, and anyone who'd rather wear the flag than fade into the background.

Matt Burlingame

Founder · Flagship Captain

Matt built Flagship Merch from a simple belief: your gear should carry the same energy as your brand. With over a decade in merchandise, printing, and brand development, he's helped local shops, touring acts, and independent crews look as sharp as they sound.

From custom hoodies to full event merch lines, every project gets the same treatment — bold design, premium materials, and a finish that holds up when it matters.
